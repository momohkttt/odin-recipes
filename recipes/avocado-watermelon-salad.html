Avocado Watermelon Salad

Description

This avocado watermelon salad is very healthy yet delightfully different. A flavorful addition could be small slices of red onion.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed watermelon
  • 4 cups fresh baby spinach, torn
  • 2 large avocados - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • ¼ cup walnut oil
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • ½ teaspoon sweet paprika

Steps

  1. Combine watermelon, spinach, and avocados in a large serving bowl.
  2. Whisk together walnut oil, olive oil, lime juice, and paprika in a small bowl until well combined; pour over watermelon mixture. Toss to coat.
